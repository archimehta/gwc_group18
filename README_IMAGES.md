# Cartoon Images Setup

To use the fun cartoon characters in EcoTrack, add your cartoon images to the `/public/earth/` folder.

## Required Files

Add these 21 PNG files to `/public/earth/`:

1. `earth-01.png` - Sun peace sign
2. `earth-02.png` - Earth holding cake
3. `earth-03.png` - Star waving
4. `earth-04.png` - Earth skateboarding
5. `earth-05.png` - Sun hugging small Earth
6. `earth-06.png` - Earth with scarf
7. `earth-07.png` - Star hands on hips
8. `earth-08.png` - Earth hugging heart
9. `earth-09.png` - Earth with snorkel
10. `earth-10.png` - Earth walking with drink
11. `earth-11.png` - Cloud character
12. `earth-12.png` - Earth waving
13. `earth-13.png` - Earth sitting with drink
14. `earth-14.png` - Earth and Sun hugging
15. `earth-15.png` - Earth meditating
16. `earth-16.png` - Earth floating in inner tube
17. `earth-17.png` - Earth biking
18. `earth-18.png` - Earth sleeping/floating
19. `earth-19.png` - Earth sticking tongue out
20. `earth-20.png` - Earth with umbrella
21. `earth-21.png` - Earth with watering can

## Where Cartoons Appear

- **Dashboard**: Earth holding cake (#1) + two additional characters
- **Log Page**: Earth and Sun hugging (#14) + Star hands on hips (#7)
- **History**: Sleeping Earth (#18) in empty state
- **Leaderboard**: Sun peace sign (#0)
- **Profile**: Meditating Earth (#15)
- **Empty States**: Random selection from all 21
